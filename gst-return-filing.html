GST Returns

Types Of GST Returns

Different GST returns are applicable based on the type of taxpayer, business category and GST compliance requirements.

GSTR-1

Return containing details of outward supplies, sales invoices and taxable transactions made during the period.

GSTR-3B

Monthly summary return containing GST liability, tax payment details and Input Tax Credit information.

GSTR-9

Annual GST return filed by registered taxpayers summarizing all GST transactions during the financial year.

CMP-08

Return applicable for taxpayers registered under the GST Composition Scheme.

Penalty & Compliance

Consequences of Late GST Return Filing

Delayed GST return filing can result in penalties, interest charges, and compliance issues that may affect your business operations.

Late Fees

GST department imposes late fees for delayed filing of returns, increasing your compliance cost.

Interest Charges

Interest may be charged on outstanding GST liabilities until payment is completed.

Input Tax Credit Loss

Delays may impact Input Tax Credit claims and create compliance complications.

Legal Notices

Continuous non-compliance can result in GST notices and additional scrutiny from authorities.
