GST Structure

What Are the Components of GST?

GST is divided into different components based on the type of transaction and location of supply.

CGST

Central Goods and Services Tax is collected by the Central Government on intra-state transactions.

SGST

State Goods and Services Tax is collected by the State Government on transactions within the same state.

IGST

Integrated Goods and Services Tax applies to interstate transactions and imports.

UTGST

Union Territory Goods and Services Tax applies to transactions in union territories.

GSTIN is a unique identification number provided to every registered business under GST.

GST Tax Slabs

What Are the GST Tax Rates?

GST rates are divided into different slabs depending on the type of goods and services.

0%

Nil Rate GST

Essential goods and services that are exempted under GST.

5%

Low Tax Slab

Applied on commonly used goods and essential services.

12%

Standard Rate

Applicable on various processed goods and services.

18%

General Rate

Most goods and services fall under this GST tax category.

28%

Higher Tax Slab

Applied on luxury items and selected products.
