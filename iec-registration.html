What Is IEC Certification?

Import Export Code (IEC) is a 10-digit identification number issued by the Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT). It is mandatory for businesses and individuals involved in importing or exporting goods and services from India.

Without an IEC Certificate, businesses cannot carry out international trade activities or receive export benefits provided by the Government of India.
