Business Setup

What is Company Registration?

Company Registration is the legal process of establishing a business entity under the Companies Act. It provides your business with a separate legal identity and helps build trust among customers, investors, and financial institutions.

A registered company can open current bank accounts, raise funding, hire employees, enter legal contracts, and expand operations across India.

Why Choose A Private Limited Company?

A Private Limited Company is one of the most preferred business structures in India. It offers limited liability protection, better credibility, easy fundraising opportunities and a separate legal identity.

Investors and financial institutions generally prefer private limited companies because of their transparency and regulated corporate structure.
