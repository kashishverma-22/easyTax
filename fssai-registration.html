Food Safety Compliance

What Is FSSAI Registration?

FSSAI Registration is a mandatory certification issued by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) for businesses involved in food manufacturing, processing, storage, distribution, transportation, or sale of food products.

It helps ensure food safety, legal compliance and enhances trust among customers and business partners.

Food License Categories

Types of FSSAI Food License in India

FSSAI provides different types of licenses based on annual turnover, business size and food business activities.

Basic Registration

Suitable for small food businesses and startups with annual turnover up to ₹12 Lakhs.

  • Small Vendors
  • Home Kitchens
  • Petty Retailers

State License

Required for medium-sized food businesses having turnover between ₹12 Lakhs and ₹20 Crore.

  • Restaurants
  • Distributors
  • Manufacturers

Central License

Mandatory for large food businesses with turnover exceeding ₹20 Crore.

  • Import Export
  • Large Industries
  • Food Chains

Special Category

Applicable to e-commerce operators, airports, seaports and special food businesses.

  • E-Commerce
  • Airports
  • Government Units

FSSAI Licence Registration Eligibility

Any individual, startup, partnership firm, company, restaurant, cloud kitchen, food manufacturer, distributor, retailer or food-related business operating in India must obtain FSSAI Registration or License based on turnover and business activities.

The type of FSSAI approval required depends on the size of the business, annual turnover, production capacity and nature of operations.

Medium Business

Large Enterprises
