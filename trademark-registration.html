About Trademark Registration

What is Trademark Registration?

Trademark Registration is a legal process that protects your brand identity and gives you exclusive rights over your business name, logo, slogan, symbol, or product name.

Secure Your Brand Before Someone Else Does

A trademark acts as a unique identity for your business. Once registered, it prevents competitors from using a similar brand name or logo that could confuse customers.

Trademark registration helps build customer trust, strengthens brand value, and provides legal protection against infringement.

Trademark Categories

Types of Trademark Registration

Different types of trademarks are available depending on the nature of your business, products, services, and branding requirements.

Word Mark

Protects a business name, brand name, or slogan in text format.

Logo Mark

Protects your company logo, symbol, design, or visual identity.

Service Mark

Used by service providers to distinguish their services.

Product Mark

Used for products to establish uniqueness and brand identity.

Collective Mark

Represents a group, association, or organization collectively.

Certification Mark

Indicates products meet specific quality or certification standards.

Required Documents

Documents Required For Trademark Registration

To complete your trademark registration process smoothly, keep the following documents ready before applying.

PAN Card

PAN card of the applicant or business entity.

Aadhaar Card

Valid identity proof of the applicant.

Address Proof

Electricity bill, voter ID, passport, etc.

Business Registration Proof

GST, MSME, Partnership Deed or Incorporation Certificate.

Logo Copy (Optional)

Required if applying for logo trademark registration.

Power of Attorney

Required when filing through a trademark consultant.
